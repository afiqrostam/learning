Hi ISIA Users,

Please be informed that we will be temporarily disabling the ISIA site from Fri Apr 27 2018 12:30:00 GMT+0800 to allow us to migrate the existing data into the new version of ISIA.

Despite of several major changes had been made; the looks and main functions of the application in the new site remains (with some exception of minor changes).

Take notice that there will be minor tweak in the profile section. If necessary, kindly make changes once the new site is published. If you’re unsure about which organization you belong to, kindly contact your local HR or send an email to us. We’re happy to assist you.

For the registered users in the Middle East and Europe, your data may not be ready when the new site is published. Rest assured, your existing data are still intact. Once we have finalized your organization structure, we will update the data into the new site and notify you accordingly.

Some of the update includes:

  • Accessibility of to submissions for all users.
  • Multiples photos attachment when submitting submission.
  • Intuitive report builder.
  • Recent location tracking for submissions.

On another note, all previous generated links from the email notification are no longer usable. Therefore, you will need to go to the site to make any update on the previous submissions.

The site is expected to be up and running again on Wed May 2 2018 21:00:00 GMT+0800.

Apologies for all the inconvenience caused.
